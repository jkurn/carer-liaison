The reality

You are the system. And when you have a bad day, everything falls apart.

You track sleep in your head. You know what he can chew. You adjust the day based on how she moved this morning. You coordinate therapy, medication, meals, naps — all from memory, notebooks, and WhatsApp screenshots. This isn't admin. It's 28 years of pattern recognition that no one else can do. But it's held by one person, with nothing underneath it.

"I know exactly what he needs. I track everything — sleep, what works, what doesn't. But none of that is written down anywhere. It's all just... me."
