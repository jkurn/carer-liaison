The reality

You are the system. And when you have a bad day, everything falls apart.

You track sleep in your head. You know what they can eat, what sets them off, what a good morning looks like. You coordinate therapy, medication, meals, naps — all from memory, notebooks, and WhatsApp screenshots. This isn't admin. It's years of pattern recognition that no one else can do. But it's held by one person, with nothing underneath it.

"I track everything — sleep, what works, what doesn't. But none of it is written down anywhere. If something happened to me, no one would know what to do."
