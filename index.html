The problem

The carer is the operating system. That's not sustainable.

When someone has full cognitive capacity but their body is the barrier, one person becomes the sole bridge between them and the world. Every decision, every plan, every communication runs through the carer. Carer Liaison changes that.

"It's not a care problem. It's a reception problem. Their mind transmits clearly. The gap is between what they transmit and what the world can receive."

What it does

Three layers. One system. Built around them.

Carer Liaison works across health, planning, and purpose — so the person you care for gets the right support, the right plan, and a reason to look forward to tomorrow.

Health Intelligence

Track body states with one button press. Monitor sleep quality via Apple Watch. The system learns what predicts a good day and adapts before you ask.

Daily Planning

Therapy matched to today's physical ability. Meal plans that balance nutrition and simplicity. Grocery lists generated automatically. The planning burden lifts.

Purpose & Ideas

Every idea captured, researched, and stored. The system finds what to look forward to each week — outings, contributions, moments of meaning. They are seen.

Built for how funding actually works

Carer Liaison components fall within existing NDIS categories. We help you build the case — from the OT assessment to the plan review conversation.

CSIRO AI-AT Framework
Cat 05 — Assistive Technology
Cat 07 — Capacity Building
  • Devices fundable under AT. Apple Watch, iPad, Stream Deck, Smart Speaker — all map to established NDIS precedent.
  • AI system setup under Capacity Building. Configuration and personalisation framed as Improved Daily Living supports.
  • OT assessment pathway included. We guide you to an OT experienced with innovative AT funding to unlock your plan.
  • Feb 2026 CSIRO framework compliant. The new AI-enabled AT framework directly supports this category of assistive technology.
