The problem

Unpaid carers do the hardest work in the country. The system barely sees them.

2.65 million Australians provide unpaid care. They track sleep patterns, adjust therapy plans, prepare meals, coordinate services, and make dozens of decisions a day — all based on knowledge that lives only in their heads. This work is invisible to the system. It's rarely funded, rarely measured, and rarely acknowledged. But without it, nothing else works.

"I know exactly what he needs. I track everything — sleep, what works, what doesn't. But none of that is written down anywhere. It's all just... me. And when I have a bad day, the whole thing falls apart."

The solution

Three layers, one system

The knowledge you carry deserves a system, not a notebook. Carer Liaison takes what you already know — the patterns, the adjustments, the daily decisions — and turns it into plans that run without you holding every detail.

Health Intelligence

Track body states, sleep quality, and daily patterns. The system learns what predicts a good day — and adapts before you even ask. One button press. One voice note. Zero friction.

Daily Planning
