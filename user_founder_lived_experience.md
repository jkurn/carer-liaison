Jonathan is building Carer Liaison from deep personal experience. His mum (60) is the primary carer for his brother (28), who is non-verbal, autistic, has global developmental delay and motoric disability, and is not toilet trained.

**Mum's reality:**
- 28 years of caring. She IS the system — when she has a bad day, everything falls apart
- Tracks nutrition, medication, bowel movements, naps, sleep, chewing constraints
- Uses notebooks, WhatsApp screenshots with annotated clocks, and her head
- Has migraines and muscle issues from the physical and emotional toll
- Communicates with brother through facilitated communication (FC) — physically exhausting
- Constantly alert, which causes her to lash out at family

**Dad (61):** Helps when he can but runs a business. Limited daily availability.

**Family situation:** Currently split between Indonesia (affordable domestic help) and Australia (better disability care). Want to get brother on NDIS but daily living costs are prohibitive. At their wits' end.

**How to apply:** This is a founder-as-user product. Every design, copy, and feature decision should pass the "mum test" — would Jonathan's mum understand it, trust it, and want to use it tomorrow? She is 60, digitally competent but not a techie, exhausted, and has no spare attention. Never treat this as a hypothetical startup — it's a real family need.
